Dr. Amandeep Jindal is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Kharagpur. He earned his Ph.D. from IIT Delhi and has held research positions at the University of Tsukuba, Japan, and Nicoya, Canada. He leads the Materials, Electrochemistry, and Microfluidics for Biomedical and Energy Research (MEMBER) Lab, focusing on advanced materials for energy storage and biomedical applications. His research interests include nanomaterials, polymeric materials, and electrochemical systems. Dr. Jindal has also collaborated with industry partners like Nippon India.
